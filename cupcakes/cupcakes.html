Steps to make a Cupcake

Step 1

Combine ingredients into a bowl and mix.

Step 2

Pour batter into a cupcake tin and place it into a preheated oven.

Step 3

Bake the cupcakes for suggested time and let it cool once it's done.

Step 4

Frost and decorate cupcakes using frosting, sprinkles, and more!

Step 5

Enjoy!
